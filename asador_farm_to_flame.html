Where fire meets the earth's finest flavours

At Asador Farm to Flame, a gastronomic experience awaits that is centred around fire — inspired by the purity of flame and the richness of natural ingredients drawn from the earth. Simple techniques, bold flavours, and authentic charcoal cooking define every plate.

The heart of the restaurant lies at the Apollo Organic Farm, the hotel's own farm which supplies the Asador daily with fresh vegetables, aromatic herbs and seasonal produce. Combined with premium meats, fresh fish and carefully selected local ingredients, the result is dishes that celebrate the authentic flavours of Corfu and the Mediterranean.

The restaurant is set in the most peaceful and tranquil corner of the resort, next to the Quiet Zone — offering a setting where natural beauty, elegance and hospitality coexist in perfect harmony.

Farm-to-Table Breakfast Experience

Every Thursday at 09:00, guests have the opportunity to visit the Apollo Organic Farm, walk through the gardens and harvest seasonal vegetables and aromatic herbs of their own choosing. The Asador chef then washes and prepares — right before their eyes — a freshly cooked omelette using the very ingredients they have just gathered from the earth, transforming a morning harvest into an authentic and unforgettable breakfast.
