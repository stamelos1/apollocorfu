The spirit of Italy, in the heart of Corfu

At the heart of Apollo Corfu, La Cantina Ristorante brings the warmth and charm of an authentic Italian trattoria — inviting guests on a gastronomic journey filled with the aromas, flavours and traditions of Italy. Inspired by the Italian philosophy of simple but exceptional cuisine, La Cantina creates dishes built on quality ingredients, fresh produce and timeless recipes.

Handmade pasta, authentic stone-baked pizzas, fine cheeses, cured meats and traditional Italian desserts compose a menu that captures the very essence of Italian gastronomy — crafted with carefully selected local ingredients from the island of Corfu.

"Simple, genuine flavours and warm hospitality await you."

The restaurant combines the rustic elegance of an Italian cantina with the relaxed atmosphere of a Corfu holiday. Warm tones, thoughtful details and a welcoming spirit create the ideal setting for a romantic dinner, a family gathering or an evening with friends — under the open sky of the amphitheatre.

At La Cantina, every meal is a celebration of Italian culture. From the first bite to the last glass of wine, guests experience the authenticity, passion and hospitality that have made Italian cuisine beloved across the world — here, at the crossroads of Italy and Corfu.

An Italian evening under the Corfu sky

La Cantina is set in the resort's beautiful open-air amphitheatre — a garden venue framed by olive trees, fragrant herbs and warm evening light. The setting brings something rare: the relaxed, unhurried soul of a true Italian trattoria, transported to the shores of Corfu.

Whether for a romantic dinner for two or a convivial table with family or friends, La Cantina offers an atmosphere that is simultaneously elegant and effortlessly welcoming — the perfect Italian evening in a Greek island setting.
