CORYFOby Ettore Botrini

Corfiot fine dining at its most authentic and refined

Perched in the most enchanting corner of the resort, where the river meets the sea and the light of Corfu colours every sunset, Coryfo by Botrini's is the ultimate gastronomic destination of Apollo Corfu. Housed in a building of authentic Corfiot architecture, the restaurant embodies the island's soul in every detail — from its setting to its cuisine.

Inspired by the culinary heritage of Corfu and guided by the philosophy of award-winning chef Ettore Botrini, the restaurant offers a contemporary interpretation of local cuisine — elevating authentic ingredients, traditional recipes and island flavours through creative, high-gastronomy dishes.

Every plate tells a story of Corfu. From aromatic herbs and outstanding local produce to traditional techniques meeting modern culinary artistry, every detail has been crafted to offer an experience that transcends a simple dinner.

"Authentic flavours. Refined execution. A true taste of Corfu."

The atmosphere of Coryfo combines understated luxury, romance and authentic hospitality. With views over the river, the lush gardens and the colours of the Ionian Sea, guests embark on a unique journey of flavours in a setting that radiates elegance and serenity.

Coryfo by Botrini's is not simply a restaurant. It is a celebration of Corfiot gastronomic heritage — an experience that connects past and present, transforming every dinner into an unforgettable memory.

👨‍🍳

Ettore Botrini

Executive Chef & Curator
★ Michelin Star

Born and raised in Corfu, Ettore Botrini is one of Greece's most celebrated chefs — the holder of a Michelin star and a passionate guardian of Corfiot gastronomic tradition.

His cuisine is rooted in the island's rich culinary heritage, shaped by Venetian influences and Greek Mediterranean character, and elevated through contemporary fine dining technique.

At Coryfo, Chef Botrini transforms the classics of Corfiot cooking — sofrito, bourdeto, pastitsada, savoro — into refined, memorable dishes that honour their origins while speaking the language of modern gastronomy.

A setting as remarkable as the cuisine

Coryfo is housed in a building of authentic Corfiot architecture — a deliberate choice that places the restaurant in dialogue with the island it celebrates. The terrace overlooks the river, the gardens and the shimmering Ionian horizon, creating one of the most romantic dinner settings in Corfu.

The name itself — Coryfo — is derived from the ancient Greek word for the twin peaks of Corfu's Old Fortress, connecting the restaurant's identity to the island's deepest history.
